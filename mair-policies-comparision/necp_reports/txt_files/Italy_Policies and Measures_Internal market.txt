Developing market coupling Market coupling will continue to be developed  it is already at full speed on the day-ahead market, and is currently evolving on the intraday markets and on the dispatching services market  as regards the implementation of the provisions set out in the applicable European network codes (the CACM and Balancing Regulations). In fact, the possibility to bid at negative prices will help tackle, according to criteria of economic merit, the excess of supply caused by the lack of flexibility in demand and/or supply of electric energy.
